    ### Round-trip with non-sensitive fields
    - test_non_sensitive_fields_in_config: save_credentials with both sensitive and non-sensitive keys; the non-sensitive key ('bucket') appears directly in get_account(); the sensitive key ('access_token') does NOT appear as plaintext in get_account()
